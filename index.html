The Studio

What makes a song good is vulnerability, emotions and creativity. It takes a kind heart to trust someone with all this.

Hektar Studios is built around one belief: your art deserves patience. From the moment you walk in — physically or virtually — you don't need to perform confidence here. Come with a rough idea, a voice note at 2am, or something in between.

There is no revision limit. There is no 'you've used your two rounds.' We stay in the process until the artist feels it — because a finished track that doesn't feel right isn't finished.

We at Hektar Studios pride ourselves to be multilingual and diverse as we Indians are known to be. So, whatever the language you speak or write in, we got you covered and we're fully open to get coached by you to understand the emotions better.
